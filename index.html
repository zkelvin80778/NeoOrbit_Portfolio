Our Mission

NeoOrbit Dynamics is a student-led innovation lab that engineers sustainable technologies for both terrestrial and extraterrestrial applications. We design resilient energy systems built for the future of the Earth—and beyond.

Projects

3D Heat Pump

This project tackles the unique challenge of habitat temperature regulation for future Martian missions. Utilizing thermoelectric generators, 3D-printed components, and open-source control systems, it aims to provide practical solutions for off-world sustainability.
Status: TRL 2
