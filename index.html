Our Mission

NeoOrbit Dynamics is a student-led innovation lab that engineers sustainable technologies for both terrestrial and extraterrestrial applications. We design resilient energy systems that are built for the future of Earth—and beyond.

Projects

3D Heat Pump

This project tackles the unique challenge of habitat temperature regulation for future Martian missions. Using thermoelectric generators, 3D-printed components, and open-source control systems, it targets practical solutions for off-world sustainability.

Status: TRL 2

Urban VAWT

This vertical-axis wind turbine project aims to empower urban buildings with renewable energy solutions. By harnessing city airflow and compact geometry, it turns skyscrapers into agents of climate action.

Status: TRL 2
