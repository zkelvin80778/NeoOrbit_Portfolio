Our Mission

NeoOrbit Dynamics pioneers next-generation solutions for sustainable engineering and extraterrestrial applications. Our work bridges renewable energy, materials science, and mechanical design — with a vision to support future Mars missions and sustainable life on Earth.

Projects

3D-Printed Solar Heat Pump

Developing a hybrid solar concentrator and thermoelectric heat pump system capable of harvesting and storing thermal energy for Martian and terrestrial environments. The system is designed with 3D-printed components and recycled materials.

View Project

Recycled PET Filament System

A 3D printer filament maker that recycles PET bottles into high-quality printing filament. Features include an automatic feed system, temperature regulation, and precision extrusion for sustainable prototyping.
